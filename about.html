Who are we?

Alpha Kappa Psi stands out as one of the premier professional business fraternities at CU Boulder. Beyond fostering professional growth and social connections, the Active Brothers of Alpha Kappa Psi are driven students, striving for excellence both within and beyond the workplace. By the history books:

Brotherhood

In 1904, Alpha Kappa Psi was founded on the principles of educating its members and the public to appreciate and demand higher ideals in business and to further the individual welfare of members during college and beyond. Alpha Kappa Psi is much more than just another organization or club—it is a unique, prestigious association of students, professors, graduates, and professionals with common interests and goals. Alpha Kappa Psi is the nation's oldest and largest professional coed business fraternity.

Building Brotherhood

Brotherhood is vitally important to AKPsi from the day members become brothers until the end of their lives. Members foster a love of the fraternity.

Lifelong Learning

In the world of business college is only the beginning. Beyond college members share their experiences and knowledge, seek out opportunities to share.

High Ethical Standards

The business world always presents decisions about how we succeed, and our membership recognizes the importance of conducting business in an ethical and legal manner.

Improving Communities

Much is expected of those to whom much is given. Improving the communities in which we live improves lives and develops goodwill.

Enhancing the Fraternity for Life

AKPsi members are given the chance to learn and experience professional conduct and given practical applications of classroom concepts.
