ALPHA KAPPA PSI

Alpha Kappa Psi stands out as one of the premier professional business fraternities at CU Boulder. Beyond fostering professional growth and social connections, the Active Brothers of Alpha Kappa Psi are driven students, striving for excellence both within and beyond the workplace. Our Chapter

We are the Gamma Zeta Chapter of Alpha Kappa Psi at the University of Colorado at Boulder. We strive to embody all core values, guiding principles, vision and ideals of Alpha Kappa Psi here in Boulder, CO. Our chapter regularly sponsors career development workshops, professional speakers and events, alumni networking and seeks to serve our community.

When you join Alpha Kappa Psi, you join an elite group of professionals who are committed to keeping their skills sharp and their careers on track. Through participation in chapter activities, you will gain critical skills that will undoubtedly set you apart from your peers in the future. More importantly, you will meet a group of students who share the same values and ambitions that you do.Â

Brotherhood

Brotherhood holds an enduring significance in AKPsi, from a member's initiation day through their entire life. We are anchored by the need to do what is in the best interest of the fraternity and act with a sense of stewardship and selflessness.
